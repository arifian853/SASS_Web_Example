Inazuma

Inazuma (Japanese: 稲妻 Inazuma) is one of the seven nations in Teyvat. It is the city-state that worships Baal, the Electro Archon, who is also the leader of its governing body, the Inazuma Bakufu.

Controlling Entity : Inazuma Bakufu
